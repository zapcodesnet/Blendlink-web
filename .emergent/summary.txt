<analysis>**original_problem_statement:**
The user's goal is to create Blendlink, a synchronized web (React) and mobile (Expo) social gaming platform. The core of the app is a photo-based game where users upload photos (Create Photo), which are then analyzed by an AI (GPT-4o) to assign a permanent Dollar Value/Power. These photos are used in a 1v1 battle system called Dollar Auction Battles.

PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS:
1.  **Synchronized Platforms**: The website and mobile app must be 100% synchronized in UI, data, and functionality, with real-time updates.
2.  **Photo Creation (Not Minting)**: A non-blockchain system where users upload photos. An AI assigns a permanent dollar value (M-B) based on visual attributes (originality, composition, lighting conditions like Sunlight, Rain, Darkness) and bonuses (human face detection). There's a daily limit of 3 free uploads for non-subscribers.
3.  **Dollar Auction Battles**: A multi-stage 1v1 battle system:
    *   **Stage 1: Million Dollar RPS Bidding Auction**: A rock-paper-scissors bidding game where players use a temporary 0M bankroll. First to 3 wins.
    *   **Stage 2: Photo Dollar Auction Clash**: A clash where the photo with the higher total Dollar Value wins.
    *   **Stage 3: Tiebreaker**: Another RPS bidding round if the first two stages have different winners.
4.  **Stamina System**: Photos have stamina, limiting the number of battles (100% = 24 battles). Stamina is lost faster on defeat and regenerates over time (1 battle per hour). Photos with 0% stamina are unusable.
5.  **Battle Selection Screen**: Before a battle, users must see a preview of their available photos, sorted by Dollar Value, showing stamina and other stats.

**User's preferred language**: English
