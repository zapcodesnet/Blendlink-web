<analysis>**original_problem_statement:**
The user's goal is to create Blendlink, a social platform with a synchronized web (React) and mobile (Expo) app.

The project underwent a major pivot away from blockchain/NFTs. The new core requirements are:
1.  **Remove All Blockchain Features**: Eliminate all code related to Immutable zkEVM, Web3, and NFT minting.
2.  **Internal Minting System**: Implement an off-chain system where users mint unique digital photo collectibles stored in MongoDB. Minting costs virtual currency (BL coins) and has daily limits.
3.  **AI-Powered Photo Game**: During minting, an AI (GPT-4o Vision) analyzes the photo to assign permanent stats (e.g., strength/weakness, originality, composition), which determines its value and performance in a game. The game includes level progression, XP, stamina, and battle mechanics (Rock-Paper-Scissors, auction battles).
4.  **Peer-to-Peer Marketplace**: A marketplace for users to sell, auction, or receive offers on their minted photos, music, and videos. A platform fee (8%) is applied to all transactions and distributed according to a referral structure.
5.  **Gamified Social Features**:
    *   **BL Coins Rewards**: A virtual currency system rewarding users for activities like posting content, commenting, and reacting. Rewards are instant, but content must remain public for 24 hours.
    *   **Custom Reactions**: A two-option system (shining golden thumbs up, silver thumbs down) with permanent reactions and BL coin rewards.
    *   **Monthly Subscriptions**: Two Stripe-based subscription tiers that provide daily BL coin bonuses and increased minting limits.
6.  **Critical Requirement**: All features, UI, and data must be 100% synchronized in real-time between the web and mobile applications.
